Multi-Role Operational Workflow Diagram

Role-Based Workflows and Cross-Role Interactions in the Academia LMS

🛡️
Administrator
System & Tenant Management
1 Tenant Configuration
Create organization, set branding and domain preferences
2 User Management
Register users, assign roles (Admin / Instructor / Learner)
3 Course Oversight
Create course shells, assign instructors to courses
4 Enrollment Control
Enroll learners into courses, manage access permissions
5 Analytics & Reports
View system-wide analytics, monitor completion rates
👨‍🏫
Instructor
Course Creation & Assessment
1 Course Design
Structure modules, define learning objectives and sequence
2 Content Upload
Upload video lectures, documents, and blog-style lessons
3 Quiz & Exam Creation
Build question banks, set time limits, enable AI proctoring
4 Grading & Feedback
Review submissions, grade assignments, provide feedback
5 Proctoring Review
Examine AI proctoring logs, review violation summaries
🎓
Learner
Course Consumption & Assessment
1 Dashboard Access
View enrolled courses, track overall progress and deadlines
2 Video Lectures
Watch tracked videos (anti-skip, 80% completion required)
3 Exam Participation
Pre-exam camera check, take AI-proctored quizzes and exams
4 Assignment Submission
Upload documents, view grades and instructor feedback
5 Certificate Download
Earn and download completion certificates upon finishing

Figure 4: Multi-role operational workflow diagram. Each role follows a distinct operational pipeline within the tenant-isolated environment.
