Figure 3: Multi-tenant request flow diagram. Tenant isolation is enforced at three critical points: (1) during credential validation at login, (2) by embedding the tenant_id inside the signed JWT token, and (3) by scoping every database query with the tenant_id extracted from the JWT middleware. No cross-tenant data leakage is possible as the tenant context is cryptographically bound to the session token.
